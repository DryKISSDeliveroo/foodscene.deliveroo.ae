  3. Eligibility:

                a. To participate in the Promotion, entrants must be a legal resident of England or United Arab Emirates and at least 18 years of age at the time of entry.

                b. A Uber Eats (UK) or Deliveroo (UAE) account is required to enter the Promotion

4.         How to enter: To enter the Promotion, entrants must complete the following steps during the Entry Period:

         a. UK: Log into the entrant's UberEats account on https://www.ubereats.com/gb or the UberEats app.

          b. UAE: Login into the entrants Deliveroo account on https://www.deliveroo.ae/   or Deliveroo app and

        c. Place a Qualifying Order.

A "Qualifying Order" means a paid-for and delivered UberEats or Deliveroo order of £10 or 50 AED amount or more (excluding tips, delivery charges and other service-related charges) placed an order over £10 or 50 AED with Sides during the Entry Period.

5.        Prizes: There are 1223 prizes to be won in this Promotion ("Prizes"). The Prizes consist of the following:

  1. 1 x Return Flight from UK to Dubai or Dubai to UK (Economy Class)
  2. 2 x 1 year Side + membership
  3. 20 x SDMN Clothing Items
  4. 200 x Sides branded T-shirts
  5. 500 x Sides branded Pin Badges
  6. 500 x Sides branded stickers

Each code will be valid for one entry, if a unique code is submitted multiple times then the Prize will be allocated to the first individual to submit the unique code.  The winners will be emailed from all eligible entries within 24 hours from submitting the form.

Sides will make reasonable efforts to contact the winner, but it is the winner's responsibility to monitor their voicemail and email account, including any junk mail folder, for receipt of the notification. To claim their prize the winner must respond  by 31st January 2022 or a new winner will be contacted.
