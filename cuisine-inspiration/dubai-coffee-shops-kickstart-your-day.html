Kickstart Your Day With A Coffee – Dubai’s Best Coffee Shops For A Tasty Blast Of Caffeine

  • 28 February 2018
  • Junaid Ahmad
  • Read time: 3 min
dubai coffee shops 112 main st bakery the coffee club more café café rider
Share this article

Is there any better way to start your day than with a rich and aromatic cup of freshly ground and roasted coffee? The classic breakfast drink perks you up for work and helps you shake off the sleepiness from the night before. In addition, it's absolutely delicious. There are so many options for coffee lovers now and Dubai is home to some exceptional traditional Arabic-style bean roasters. So if you're looking for the perfect breakfast partner to see you through the morning, check out these coffee shops in the buzzing city.  

1. 112 Main St Bakery

A post shared by 112 Main St. Bakery (@112bakery) on Nov 24, 2017 at 2:49am PST

Situated in Emaar Business Park, 112 Main St Bakery specialises in creating some of the most stunning artisan baked goods in Dubai. Everything is prepared fresh each day, using only the finest ingredients. You're sure to find something to munch on for your morning meal at this great little eatery, but we're here to talk about the main event – the coffee. The quaint bakery serves over ten different varieties, hot or cold depending on your mood.  One of our favourites is the turmeric latte, an exquisite and innovative Middle Eastern twist on the traditional. A must try!

2. The Coffee Club

A post shared by The Coffee Club Middle East (@thecoffeeclubme) on Feb 23, 2018 at 8:40pm PST

We're all about the beans right now and The Coffee Club does exactly what it says on the tin, serving up some of the best in the UAE. There are also some sensational salads, sandwiches and other treats for the taste buds on the agenda here. In terms of what to select for your epic morning wake up call, there are more than twenty items to choose from. You'll never get bored ordering from here! How about going for the light and milky Café Latte? Or if you have a sweet tooth then look no further than the marvellous Mocha. These large coffees will surely set you in the right frame of mind for the day ahead.

3. More Café

A post shared by • Ĵō ŚВ • (@curly_jo77) on Mar 3, 2017 at 11:13pm PST

The extensive menu at More Café aims to take international dishes and present them in a new and innovative way. This includes an eclectic mix of delicious items for any meal time – but it's their coffees that made them famous. There are some highly original twists on the beans here and if you fancy trying something new, this is the place to go. We absolutely love the Ristretto Romano with a hint of lemon and vanilla. We're sure you'll also go wild for the frothy Mochaccino.

4. Al Barza

A post shared by I Photograph My Daily Food (@arafoodgrapher) on Sep 2, 2016 at 6:11am PDT

If you want to sample some tantalising traditional Arabic coffee, get down to Al Barza, which is one of the most popular breakfast joints in the city. Try out the aromatic and bold flavours of the beans in the Arabic Coffee. Alternatively, you could go for the familiar with a cappuccino or latte. Either way, you'll love the freshness of the fare here.

Get your day off to the perfect start by ordering one of these fresh and delicious coffees. They can all be dropped directly at your door with Deliveroo.
