From Steak To Oysters, Fancy Meat And Seafood For The One You Fancy

  • 22 February 2018
  • Faizan Ali
  • Read time: 4 min
steak seafood italian world foods european american dubai vegetarian
Share this article

Isn't it about time you start looking at ways to treat the one you love to something special? What better way to show them how much they mean to you than by treating them to a slap-up meal fit for a king or queen?

From the best ways to eat oyster seafood to the finest cuts of steak and from American to Chinese, there are so many opulent restaurants in Dubai which serve up the fanciest foods without the huge price tag. So let's dive right in and see what you could be treating your loved one to on your next date night.

1. Ribeye Wagyu Steak

A post shared by Uno Chicago Grill, UAE اونو (@unosuae) on Jan 31, 2016 at 1:21am PST

When it comes to fancy foods, you really can't go wrong with a good steak. The Ribeye Wagyu Steak served at UNO Chicago Grill uses 12oz of the best wagyu beef which is chargrilled to perfection and seasoned to give it even more delicious flavour and tenderness. Each bite is so tender and juicy and you'll be left truly wowed at just how tasty this dish is. Be warned, once your date gets a taste they will keep coming back for more!

2. Stir Fry Fresh Squid

A post shared by Mc Pury Gutierrez (@iampurygutz) on Oct 29, 2016 at 3:54pm PDT

It doesn't get much more posh than oysters, used in many of the most luxurious European dishes. Oysters are the unlikely star of the delicious Stir Fry Fresh Squid served at China Times. This tasty Chinese dish is made with a delicious stir-fried squid, premium oysters, garlic, Szechuan and Manchurian sauce and a black bean paste as well as your own choice of their homemade sauces to go with it. Some may be apprehensive about trying squid, but rest assured it is a truly delicious treat and it is a decision you will not regret.

3. Joe's Lobster Thermidor

A post shared by JOE'S Cafe Dubai (@joescafedubai) on Feb 15, 2017 at 4:33am PST

Joe's Cafe knocks it out of the park every time, and their Lobster Thermidor is no exception. This classic French dish uses only the best lobster meat which is cooked in a wonderfully creamy sauce and served in a lobster shell along with tasty vegetables and a lemon wedge to drizzle over. Whether you're a seasoned fan of lobster or a newbie all the way, this lobster is truly some of the best you will find in the city, so make sure you try it with your loved one (or alone, we won't tell on you!).

4. Truffle and Mushroom Risotto

A post shared by Jones the Grocer (@jonesuae) on May 22, 2016 at 5:54am PDT

If you are living the meat-free life but still want to eat fancy, go for the Truffle and Mushroom Risotto served at Jones the Grocer. This restaurant uses the finest truffles and tastiest mushrooms to make its risotto. Enjoy perfectly sauteed mixed mushrooms and a delicious white truffle oil drizzled over it. Each bite of this meal is an absolute delight and you will be certain to come back and enjoy it all over again.

Treating your significant other to a slap-up meal with all of the fancy trimmings is a perfect way to make your romantic date nights special. You both deserve a night to splash out and taste the finer things in life, so be sure to try these delicious meals next time you are feeling hungry and make it a night to remember.
