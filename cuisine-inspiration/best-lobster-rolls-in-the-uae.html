5 of the best lobster rolls in the UAE

  • 18 July 2017
  • Editor
  • Read time: 3 min
uae lobster lobster rolls
Share this article

Warm, succulent and comforting, we can only be talking about the iconic lobster roll, right? This sassy little sandwich is great for snacking on with friends, a snazzy lunch, or to enjoy to yourself on an indulgent night in. Once a New England staple – and now an international favourite – there's all sorts of places serving up lobster rolls here in the UAE, so if you're looking for some of the best, here's five to try out first.

1. Burger & Lobster

A post shared by Burger & Lobster Dubai (@burgerandlobsterdubai) on May 14, 2017 at 1:26am PDT

When a restaurant has a couple of killer staples, you know they're going to be good. And these guys are known for their awesome lobster rolls. Here you'll find fresh lobster meat tossed in a zingy Japanese mayo dressing and topped with fresh chives, served up with a side of fries and crisp salad, you get the best balance of doughy, meaty, crunchy goodness.

Where: Burger & Lobster, Dubai

2. Rock & Rolls

A post shared by Rock & Rolls (@rocknrollsuae) on Feb 5, 2017 at 3:29am PST

At Rock & Rolls, they take their rolls seriously. And you can taste this dedication in every bite. These guys are even giving a new style a shot too –  instead of fried lobster chunks in your classic roll, find it served up grilled, drizzled in a light sauce and decorated with pickles. And the good news doesn't stop there, these grilled delights are much healthier than its fried cousin.

Where: Rock & Rolls, Abu Dhabi

3. Urban Seafood

A post shared by Urban Seafood (@urbanseafood) on Jan 28, 2017 at 2:03am PST

Sometimes, you get a roll that looks just as good as it tastes. And you get just that over at Urban Seafood, with each dish more Instagram worthy than the last. Here a light and fluffy freshly baked roll is sliced at the top and stuffed with pure Maine lobster meat. Seasoned with lemon like the classic, but sporting a Jersey sauce, it's a fresh take on everyone's favourite.

Where: Urban Seafood, Dubai

4. Joe's Crab Shack

A post shared by Joes Crab Shack Uae (@jcsuae) on May 9, 2017 at 4:42am PDT

When a lobster roll at lunchtime calls, Joe's Crab Shack has you covered. A classic slit top roll is first stuffed with a luscious lettuce garnish, before the staple takes centre stage. Incredible juicy and tiny lobster chunks are lightly tossed in a mayo dressing and decorated with celery. With fries and house coleslaw on the side, it's always a treat-worthy option to order.

Where: Joe's Crab Shack, various locations

5. Senara

For times when you want a lobster roll and then some, Senara's the spot to hit up. Serving the signature roll as a starter, you get the same powerful taste, balanced perfectly with a bell pepper salad on the side. But don't be fooled into thinking this snack is simple – complete with a punchy jalapeño, it's got substance and style.

Where: Senara, Dubai
