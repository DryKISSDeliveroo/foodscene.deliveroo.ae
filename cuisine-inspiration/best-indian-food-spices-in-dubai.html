2.Bombay Bungalow

A post shared by Bombay Bungalow (@bombaybungalow) on Sep 14, 2017 at 7:01am PDT

Founded by local Dubai chefs, Bombay Bungalow aims to deliver modern Indian food made with traditional Indian recipes. Their menu is extremely unique as well as contemporary, as every classic dish comes with a twist. The food is so good that even the founders have their favourite dishes, with Fadi Haider preferring the ghee roasted coconut crab, while Marwan Istaitie believes they serve the best butter chicken in the whole of Dubai. Of course, there's only one way you can find out for yourself - order from these guys today!

Where:Â Bombay Bungalow, Jumeirah Beach Residence

3.Curry Culture

A post shared by Curry Culture (@curryculture) on Sep 10, 2017 at 2:03am PDT

If you fancy some traditional homemade meals with a fine dining twist, Curry Culture is the place to go. Their menu is packed with authentic Indian meals that are arranged to take you on a flavor journey. It begins with starters including ganga jamuna, tikka cholley and shammi kebab, then continues with "Mom's Kitchen" as the founders loved their mothers' recipes. If you're still hungry you can choose from the dhabbas of India, get a tandoori kabab or go for classic curries and biryanis. The choice is all yours.
