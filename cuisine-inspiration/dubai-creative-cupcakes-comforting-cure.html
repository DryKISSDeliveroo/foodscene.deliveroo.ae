1. Vanilla Classic Cupcake

Sometimes you just want to taste the familiar flavours of a cupcake that features on bakery menus all over the world. At Magnolia Bakery, there are various offerings to choose from, but their take on the classic vanilla cupcake is one of the stand-out options. This light and fluffy treat is smothered in delicious vanilla frosting and can be finished with decorations of your choice. Other tempting offers on the menu here include the Red Velvet and the Winter Spice cupcakes.

3. Peanut Butter Nutella Cupookie

A cupookie is a genius hybrid dessert between two of the best pudding items of all time - cupcakes and cookies. AtÂ Sugarmoo, the innovative chefs are often trying out new combinations, and these sometimes run as weekly and monthly specials. Try one of these today, and you may have found a new favourite dessert item.
