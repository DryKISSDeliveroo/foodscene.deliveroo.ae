Feed My Valentine - Best Cooked Sushi Foods To Eat On Date Night

  • 18 February 2018
  • Faizan Ali
  • Read time: 3 min
sushi sashimi japanese food seafood world foods dubai
Share this article

Valentine's Day is fast approaching and one way to bring the romance is by sharing some finger foods with your date. It has to be said that sushi foods are some of the best around. You or your date may be apprehensive about eating raw fish but sushi doesn't have to mean eating uncooked seafood and there are plenty of cooked ways to enjoy it that are just as delicious, if not more so. Once you have discovered just how tasty sushi can be, then you'll be far more willing to try out all of the wonderful kinds of sushi, cooked or not! So, be sure to make sushi your date food choice this Valentine's Day and treat yourself. To help you, we have made a list of some of the best cooked sushi in Dubai to make your date night one to remember: let's take a look.

1. Tori Teriyaki Roll

A post shared by ItadakiJapaneseRestaurant (@itadakijapaneserestaurant) on Jul 27, 2017 at 1:18am PDT

Whether you are just making your foray into the world of oriental nibbles or are a seasoned pro, you are sure to enjoy the Tori Teriyaki Roll served at Itadaki Japanese Restaurant. This sushi roll is made with teriyaki chicken, cream cheese, asparagus and avocado and it is a simple yet sumptuous way to eat this treat. The flavours compliment each other perfectly and will keep you diving in for more.

2. Crazy California

For the perfect dish to share with your date, go for the Crazy California rolls at Le Gourmet Signature. This classic sushi dish is always a good idea, as it is made with crab, lettuce, avocado, mayonnaise and tobiko and each bite is a total delight. This 16-piece sushi dish is enough to satisfy both you and your date while still leaving room for dessert, so be sure to give it a try and you won't regret it!

3. Lion King Roll

A post shared by miyabi sushi (@miyabi_sushi) on Nov 10, 2017 at 11:33pm PST

If you are looking for something that packs more of a punch, try out the Lion King Roll from Miyabi Sushi and Bento. This sushi is also a California roll however it is topped with delicious baked salmon, spicy mayonnaise, lemon cream, mozzarella and teriyaki sauce: together they make a dish that is brimming with wonderful flavours.

4. Rotagilla

A post shared by SAMURAI SUSHI DXB (@samuraisushidxb) on Jun 2, 2017 at 12:08am PDT

For something a little bit different, why not try out the Rotagilla maki roll served at Samurai Sushi and Grill Restaurant? This sushi roll is made with shrimp tempura, cucumber, topped with tobiko and served with a sweet chilli sauce and spicy mayo. This tasty dish gives you plenty of flavour and is by no means a boring choice. You'll be savouring every last bite and enjoy every second of eating this meal, though be careful when sharing this with your date as they may want to steal it from you!
