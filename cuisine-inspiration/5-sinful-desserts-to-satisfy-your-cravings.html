5 Sinful Desserts to Satisfy Your Cravings

  • 14 June 2016
  • Mahek Punjabi
  • Read time: 3 min
desserts
Share this article

You may not be able to buy happiness, but what you CAN do is Deliveroo some guilty pleasures to your door...

Check out these 5 sinful desserts, best enjoyed in your pyjamas.  Don't worry though - our riders won't judge!

1. Glorious Gelato

A good ol' classic to lift your mood; chocolate and hazelnut gelato with chocolate ganache and dark cocoa… cool, creamy chocolatey heaven.

We suggest: Tartufo Nero | Rossovivo Artisan Pizza

[Delivers around Media City and Downtown Dubai]

2. Peanut Butter Goodness

You don't need to wait for your birthday to order a peanut butter cake.  Besides, it's somebody's birthday... somewhere.  Peanut butter can make a perfectly flavored impulsive snack to cheer you up.

We Suggest: Peanut Butter Jar | Molten Me

[Delivers around Jumeirah]

3. Cinnamon Rolls

Cinnamon rolls on the couch = bliss!  Freshly baked, coated with cinnamon and drizzled with chocolate and nuts. YUM! #PMSBuster

What to Try: Pecabon | Cinnabon

[Delivers around Jumeirah, Jumeirah Beach Residence, Dubai Marina, Al Barsha, Dubai Mall]

4. Cookie Cravings

Love cookies?  Love brownies?  How about a big chocolate chip cookie filled with brownies? #MindBlown

Bet eating cookies straight outta the box sounds like a pretty #basic idea now...

We Suggest: Cookiesaurus | Brownie Box

[Delivers around Al Wasl]

5. Brownie Madness

A staple, how could  not make this list?  Really do make the best impulsive cheat meals, and there's nothing else quite as satisfying!

We Suggest: Yolo Bar | Sugar Moo

[Delivers around Jumeirah and Al Quoz]
