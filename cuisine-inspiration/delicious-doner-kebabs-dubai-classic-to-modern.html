Don’t miss out on these delicious doners in Dubai

  • 6 April 2018
  • Manar Khan
  • Read time: 4 min
doner kebab lebanese food middle eastern european meat street food world foods
Share this article

Doner kebabs are a Middle Eastern-inspired lunch dish that's hugely popular across Europe and is now making waves in Dubai. The doner was actually invented in Berlin back in the 1960s by Turkish immigrants and they have taken the world by storm. Whether it's for a messy late-night meal or an al fresco lunch, there is no denying that doners are truly delicious. Dubai is loving Lebanese food and the Doner craze that comes with it, so let's take a look at some of the best doner kebabs in town.

1. Grand Berlin Doner

A post shared by ♛ Ƶαкι Ƶαcнαℜιαн ♛ (@zaq_thequirkylad) on May 13, 2017 at 3:40am PDT

It doesn't get more authentic than the Grand Berlin Doner, served at Doner and Gyros. This dish gives you the classic Berlin Doner Kebab without having to make the trip to Germany. It's made with your choice of meat – either a succulent chicken or beef – and a generous portion of fresh crispy lettuce. Tomatoes, onion and red cabbage are added before it's all topped with their in-house custom sauce blends and served in a crispy flatbread. This is a great lunch option to keep you full and provide you with a delicious flavourful experience.

2. Health in a Box

A post shared by Doner Deli دونر ديلي (@donerdeli) on Aug 16, 2017 at 4:30am PDT

Some of the best food you can try is street food, however it doesn't always have to be unhealthy. The Health in a Box Doner Kebab from Doner Deli is a much healthier alternative that won't make you feel guilty for breaking your diet. Instead of getting your hands messy with a flatbread or wrap, this doner is served in a box, making it a little easier to eat. Whether you choose veal, chicken or a mixed doner meat, it's served with green salad including tomatoes, lettuce, onion and red cabbage, garlic mayonnaise and a creamy yoghurt sauce. There is a reason this is one of the most popular items on the menu, so be sure to get your hands on this dish for your next lunch break.

3. Veggie Doner

A post shared by Umar Bassam (@umar_bassam) on Mar 17, 2018 at 11:49am PDT

There is no need for vegetarians to miss out on the doner fun, as the guys at German Doner Kebab have created a Veggie Doner Sandwich that's just as delicious. Instead of meat, this doner is made with lots of fresh veggies such as lettuce, onion, tomato, red cabbage and not one, not two but three different special sauces that are spicy, yoghurt and garlic in flavour – for that extra kick. This dish does a great job of showing that not eating meat doesn't mean you have to miss out!

4. Kafta Lita

A post shared by Manoushe Street (@manoushe_street) on Mar 7, 2018 at 12:00am PST

The Kafta Lita Kebab served at Man'oushe Street is absolutely brimming with wonderful flavours. It's made with Lebanese-style beef, packed with crisp fresh lettuce and topped with a mouth-wateringly creamy hummus, pickles and tomatoes. This authentic taste of Lebanon is just what you need when lunchtime comes calling, so be sure to pick one up and become the envy of the office!

Doner kebabs are a great way to feed a hungry stomach and they are an absolute treat to behold. You can easily eat these on the go or sit down and savour every last bite, so make sure you get your hands on a doner today!
