Top 10 Pizza toppings not to be missed in the UAE

  • 29 September 2017
  • Manar Khan
  • Read time: 4 min
pizza uae dubai abu dhabi pizza toppings italian bacon mushrooms calamari gorgonzola provola
Share this article

Whether you're a veggie lover or a meat feast fanatic, everyone loves pizza. If you're looking for a great pizza topping in the United Arab Emirates we've found the best out there from Dubai to Abu Dhabi. Let's have a look at the top 10 pizza toppings in the UAE and where to get your dose of these mouthwatering, cheesy delights.

1. The Hawaiian, Jazz@PizzaExpress

A post shared by Time Out Abu Dhabi Food (@timeoutabudhabi_food) on Mar 8, 2017 at 1:13am PST

Mainstream Italian food is the bread and butter of most PizzaExpress restaurants, but in this Abu Dhabi venue they've decided to add their own twist to the traditional Hawaiian. Instead of fruit chunks, this pizza has spicy pineapple salsa combined with veal bacon. Tell us more, Jazz!

Where to grab a slice: Jazz@PizzaExpress, Abu Dhabi

2. Margherita, Carluccios

A post shared by Chasing Wonders (@chasingwondersnet) on Mar 2, 2015 at 6:44am PST

Sure, at first glance you may not be too enticed by a pretty normal-looking margherita pizza, but what you don't know is that Carluccio's uses a unique, tangy tomato base. Somehow, they've managed to make a margherita interesting again, and that deserves some applause.

Where to grab a slice: Carluccios, Dubai Marina Mall

3. Truffle Goodness, Pinza

A post shared by Pinza!® !بينزا® (@itspinza) on Jun 27, 2017 at 12:39am PDT

When it comes to vegetarian pizza options, most are usually made up of different types of cheese. Not this pizza at Pinza, which is topped with gourmet mushrooms (including truffles), though there is also some yummy burrata.

Where to grab a slice: Pinza, Abu Dhabi

4. Smoked Provola & Ham, 800Pizza

A post shared by 800PIZZA Abu Dhabi (@800pizzaabudhabi) on Oct 10, 2016 at 6:30am PDT

It's true that 800Pizza's menu is primarily filled with vegetarian-friendly options, but for the meat loving diners, there's the smoked provola and turkey ham topping. This delicious combination is accompanied by Italian mozzarella on a thin and crispy base.

Where to grab a slice: 800Pizza, Abu Dhabi

5. Proscuitto e Funghi, Mamma Italia

A post shared by Mamma Italia (@mammaitaliauae) on Apr 2, 2015 at 1:24am PDT

With mozzarella cheese, proscuitto, turkey ham, mushrooms and mixed herbs the Proscuitto e Funghi is a must-have. It's incredibly popular, though the spicy Diavola looks good too.

Where to grab a slice: Mamma Italia, Dubai

6. Mediterranean, NKD Pizza

A post shared by NKD Pizza 🍕 (@nkdpizza) on Mar 8, 2017 at 10:13am PST

If you're looking for an eclectic pizza menu, gluten-free, health-conscious pizzeria NKD Pizza has you covered. Their Mediterranean is topped with artichoke, sun-dried tomato, onion, black olives and loads of feta cheese, and comes in three different sizes.

Where to grab a slice: NKD Pizza, Dubai Business Bay

7. Pepperoni, The Pizza Factory

A post shared by The Pizza Factory (@the.pizza.factory) on Nov 9, 2016 at 4:27am PST

Although this establishment may look like your everyday pizzeria, at The Pizza Factory they use a innovative volcano stone oven to cook every single pizza and use flavours from around the world. Don't believe us? Just try their pepperoni.

Where to grab a slice: The Pizza Factory, Dubai

8. Ultimate, Shakespeare & Co

A post shared by Shakespeare and Co. (@shakespeareandco) on Sep 4, 2017 at 10:52pm PDT

There's so much to choose from at Shakespeare & Co. that their pizzas sometimes go unnoticed. However, everyone must try their Ultimate pizza, as it is covered in everything you could want from a pizza. Mozzarella, pepperoni, chicken fajita, mixed capsicum, tomatoes, mushroom, shallots, olives and oregano to name a few.

Where to grab a slice: Shakespeare & Co, Dubai

9. La Frutti Mare, Certo Italian

A post shared by Piece of my life ✨ (@koricaki90) on Jul 29, 2016 at 2:26pm PDT

Of course, meat-heavy pizzas aren't for everyone – some people prefer a little light seafood. That's where Cerco Italian's La Frutti Mare hits the spot, with calamari, shrimps and mussels.

Where to grab a slice: Certo Italian, Dubai

10. 4 Formaggi, Rossovivo Artisan Pizza

A post shared by Rossovivo Artisan Pizza (@rossovivopizza) on May 10, 2017 at 11:10pm PDT

For cheese lovers, a classic four cheese pizza is the best. So, why not get the 4 formaggi from Rossovivo's? Mozzarella, gorgonzola, emmental, parmesan all mixed together to create one great pizza.

Where to grab a slice: Rossovivo Artisan Pizza, Dubai

We're going to admit it: Here at the Deliveroo offices, we got so tempted writing this guide that we've already ordered pizzas (and it's not even lunch time!) Which one would you like? Grab your pizza on Deliveroo.
