Vibrant vegan poké bowls that will make your mouth water in Dubai

  • 14 March 2018
  • Manar Khan
  • Read time: 4 min
poke bowls vegan vegetarian healthy food japanese sushi foodies food trends dubai
Share this article

Living the vegan lifestyle can often be tricky. Finding meals to suit your diet isn't always straightforward and sometimes vegan options just aren't exciting. However, with the new poké craze, more and more vegan options are opening up. Poké bowls are a Hawaiian speciality which are also hugely popular in Japan, and they have now made their way to Dubai, much to our delight. The dish is usually made with raw fish, however they are hugely customisable and can be made with whichever ingredients you like. Without further ado, let's explore the best vegan poké bowls you can find right here in Dubai today.

1. Lanai

A post shared by Poké and Co Dubai (@pokeandco) on Aug 24, 2017 at 10:35pm PDT

For a meal that leaves you feeling healthy, energised and full, be sure to go for the Lanai poke bowl from Poke and Co. This dish is made with only the freshest and healthiest veggies, which will have you feeling healthier with each bite, including mushrooms, edamame, spring onions, radish, avocado and coriander. It's also topped with tasty tofu and pineapple and sprinkled with matcha miso sauce and chilli flakes for a little spicy kick. Whoever said that vegan meals are flavourless clearly hasn't experienced the Lanai bowl, as this dish definitely proves them wrong!

2. Build Your Own Bowl

A post shared by Malolo Dubai (@malolodubai) on Feb 24, 2018 at 1:08am PST

Sometimes when you order a meal there are items you wish you could have added to it or taken away from it, which is why the Build Your Own Bowl from Malolo Poke is perfect. This dish allows you to simply choose whichever ingredients you'd like, so there are no unpleasant surprises or missing items. Start with your base and choose from white rice, quinoa, wakame or kale. Then, move on to your choice of sauce, where the vegan options include teriyaki sauce or soy and lime. From there you just add whichever four veggies you'd like, from cabbage to cucumber, avocado to edamame and so many more. Then you just add your toppings which include a range of nuts, kale, onions, leeks and more. This dish is perfect, as you can truly make it your own and ensure that there are only ingredients in there you would enjoy eating.

3. Super Bowl

A post shared by Σπυριδουλα Ειρηνη (@miss_eirini) on Mar 5, 2018 at 6:31am PST

Get yourself a well and truly wholesome and healthy meal with the Super Bowl made over at Circle Cafe. This dish is brimming with the healthiest and most delicious ingredients to make a lunch which will keep you full of energy and raring to go. It's made with a tasty beetroot hummus, a quinoa base, edamame, kale, falafel, cucumber, roasted pumpkin, sweet cherry tomatoes, Cajun bagel chips and spinach, along with a sprinkling of sunflower seeds and a drizzling of honey soya dressing to really help bring the flavours out. This bowl is sure to have you feeling good, so if you need a pick me up, this is the perfect meal for you.

4. Vegetable Poke

A post shared by Sushi Counter (@sushi_counter) on Jan 3, 2018 at 8:30am PST

The Vegetable Poke served over at Sushi Counter is the perfect example of great vegan food. It's made with good, fresh, plant-based ingredients which are crisp and delicious to eat. There is a rice base which is topped with carrot, cucumber, edamame, almond flakes, avocado, pickled ginger, radish, tempura flakes, coconut puree and a drizzling of sweet chilli sauce. This bowl is so packed with flavour you won't believe you're actually eating something so healthy!

Poke bowls are well and truly taking over Dubai and being vegan doesn't mean you have to miss out. With so many tasty plant-based options out there, you'll be able to find the perfect poke for you with no effort at all.
