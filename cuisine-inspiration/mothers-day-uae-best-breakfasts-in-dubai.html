Get Mother’s Day in the UAE off to a cracking start this year and match the most important meal to your Mum’s personality

  • 18 March 2018
  • Faizan Ali
  • Read time: 4 min
mother's day breakfasts pancakes eggs mexican food dessert
Share this article

Mother's Day is just around the corner so it's time to show your Mum how much she means to you: spoil her rotten with good nosh. What makes our Mums so special is their lovely personalities, so why not get them a breakfast to match? Dubai has some fantastic breakfast restaurants, so whether she is a sophisticated lady, an outgoing party animal, a quiet and gentle homebody or anything in between, we are here to help you find the perfect breakfast for your perfect mother.

1. Sophisticated

A post shared by Yoti ♫ ♡ (@hayatbaraiyan) on Jan 27, 2018 at 12:51am PST

Those sophisticated mammas out there deserve to be treated like royalty, so make them feel special with the Eggs Royale served at L'ETO. You can't get much more delicious than runny poached eggs served on homemade spinach buns with herb-cured salmon and creamy hollandaise sauce. This dish takes Eggs Benedict to the next level and each bite is so tasty your Mum will be blown away. So why would you start her day in any other way?

2. Outgoing

A post shared by Taqado Mexican Kitchen (@taqadomexicankitchen) on Oct 7, 2017 at 11:41pm PDT

A diverse range of tastes and cuisines from all over the world means Dubai has some of the best breakfasts around. For the daughter with an outgoing Mum, give her a taste of Mexico with the Huevos Rancheros served over at Taquado Mexican Kitchen. This delicious dish is the ideal wake up call on account of its huge kick of flavour, as it's made with chilli con carne and organic fried eggs and served in soft tacos topped with salsa roja. This dish is due to become a fast favourite, so make sure you have these guys bookmarked – your Mum is sure to ask for them again!

3. Indulgent

A post shared by Clinton Street Baking Company (@clintonstdubai) on Feb 18, 2018 at 9:24pm PST

If your Mum loves to indulge in the finer things in life, make sure to get her the Pancakes with Warm Maple Butter and Chocolate Chunks from Clinton St. Baking Company. These pancakes are the ultimate satisfaction for anyone with a sweet tooth. Soft and fluffy pancakes are drizzled in the most delicious maple butter and topped with tasty chocolate chips. If chocolate chunks aren't her thing, they can be substituted with wild Maine blueberries, banana or walnuts. Either way, they will certainly make the first meal of the day the best one.

4. Classic

A post shared by The Victorian (@thevictorianuae) on Nov 9, 2017 at 8:38pm PST

For Mums who love to keep it classic, the Full Victorian from The Victorian is where it's at. This is the Queen of all breakfasts: the classic full English breakfast, made up of fried eggs, baked beans, sausages, bacon and hash browns along with a cup of tea and orange juice to wash it all down. Each bite is so tasty you can bet there won't be a morsel left on the plate when she's through with it. It may not be the healthiest option but it's surely one of the tastiest and is sure to make you very popular with her on Mother's Day!

Your Mum is a very special lady who deserves a very special day, and there is no better way to kick that day off than with these wonderful breakfasts. Give her a treat she will absolutely love and keep her taste buds happy with the best breakfast ever.
