Hot scoop! What’s happening in Deliveroo Editions?

  • 11 April 2018
  • Marni Immelman
  • Read time: 8 min
dubai uae
Share this article

You might not know this, but Deliveroo Editions is pretty awesome (if we may say so ourselves). We've brought in 13 new restaurants to 'new Dubai' and given them a home in our JLT Deliveroo Editions site. For you, this means that you can get awesome food from restaurants that weren't previously available to you. And it goes without mentioning; Editions is Deliveroo's seal of excellence. We're talking faster delivery, yummy food with the highest rankings, and more offers than anywhere else. This is what you can look out for from Deliveroo Editions in 'new Dubai'

Hen House

Hen House's delicious Southern fried chicken is an absolute winner. With a variety of fried chicken meal options, your taste buds will feel like they are in the Southern states of the USA.

Bayou BBQ

A black Angus beef rib and a short rib sandwich are first on the menu and with good reason. They are mouth-wateringly delicious and slow-cooked to perfection. But don't forget Bayou BBQ's other rib meals, burgers and tasty desserts.

Brickyard Burger

Brickyard's signature burgers are crispy, juicy and seasoned perfectly. They use high-quality ingredients and you can even build your own burger. Their milkshakes are just as amazing and come in a variety of different flavours that are the perfect compliment to a perfect burger.

Clinton St. Baking Company

The top-pick at this delivery-only restaurant would definitely be their fried chicken waffles which are served with a honey-tabasco sauce and warm maple butter. If you are ordering breakfast, then it is a definite must. But also look out for Clinton St. Baking Company's signature pancakes (with a variety of fillings) or their brioche french toast, all served with warm maple butter.

Pinza

A Pinzafied-Margherita pizza, Truffle-goodness or a plain Pepperoni pizza are some of the top picks at this restaurant. But there is nothing plain about these pizzas. They are oozing with cheesy-goodness and come hot to your door. They use the freshest ingredients and are of top-quality.

The Bright Kitchen

The Bright Kitchen makes delicious and nutritious meals and snacks for the health-conscious foodie. They keep calories in mind but they never compromise taste. Look out for their oatmeal chia that is filled with antioxidants and omega 3's, and it is 100% vegan as well as their lowest-calorie salad, the Mexicano.

Barsalata

Barsalata offers signature salads that consist of a blend of Eastern and Western ingredients and bursts with innovation and taste. The dressings that go with the salads are scrumptious and deeply enhance their super-fresh ingredients. Their flourless-brownie is also delightful and guilt-free and will fill up the room with a rich smell of chocolate. Don't forget to get an extra vitamin fix with their orange glory, super detox and green glow juices.

Burger & Lobster

Burgers and lobsters...need we say more? Burger and Lobster is the perfect place for good quality comfort food for those days your craving a cosy night in. The original lobster roll is the one that started it all. It consists out of fresh lobster meat served within two rolls, tossed in Japanese Mayo and topped with chives and are accompanied by fries and fresh salad.

Rawdium

All raw ingredients, and all extremely nutritious. Skip the diet and try Rawdium's guilt-free dishes. You are set for breakfast, lunch, dinner and dessert. Their Nutri-Bullet breakfast is extremely appetising, and they even offer mini-pizzas and pasta.

Cali-Poke

This poke-bowl master has some of the top-selling dishes in the UAE. Cali-Poke is one of Dubai's favourite poke-spots with poke-bowls that will satisfy both Mexican food and sushi lover's cravings.

Hippy Deli

The Hippy Deli uses 100% plant-based ingredients, offering wholesome and nutritious meals for athletes, yoga enthusiasts, busy bees and literally everyone in between. If the vegan lifestyle is something you live by then Hippy Deli is definitely the place for you. Top picks include acai bowls, Buddha bowls and their banana oats vegan french toast served with a choice of peanut butter toffee or dark chocolate sauce.

Vurger

Vegan Burgers never looked this good, with a range of super colourful and super tasty burgers. Try Vurger's delicious Vegetarian style Korean BBQ burger.  It's made with a chickpea patty that consists of brown rice, sweet potatoes, celery, mushrooms, rolled oats, and millet seeds topped with avocado, vegan mayo and kimchi, in a beetroot bun. With so many flavours you will definitely be drooling.

Hoku Poke

There is a new kid on the editions block and it's called Hoku Poke. Build your own Poke bowl or your own Acai bowl. Or order any of their signature Poke bowls such as the dynamite prawns poke or their Japanese teriyaki maguro. These dishes are super fresh and seriously mouth-watering.

Fraiche

Fraiche prides itself on bringing you delicious dishes with a twist. Offering you the best of both worlds when it comes to cuisine in the UAE, try their Avo-corner, beef bourguignon, cornflake chicken burger or a delicious chorizo burger.

Dip Street

What do you get when you combine mouthwatering sandwiches and some awesome sauces? Try Dip Street's grilled mac and cheese sandwich with a fresh homemade tomato dip or a classic roast beef and melted cheese on sourdough bread, served with beef Au Jus dip. This is comfort food at it's best.  

Wild & the Moon

Wild & the Moon offer 100% vegan, gluten-free, organic, plant-based dishes and beverages. Unique to their menu is their vitality shots and cold pressed juices. They also offer a cute kids smoothy namely, the 'big and strong' smoothy. Also if you are looking for some wholesome snacks try their veggie crackers, popcorn, dried kale chips, or their raw granola.

With quicker delivery times and an abundance of tasty options, Deliveroo Editions is an excellent choice for breakfast, lunch and dinner. So what will have to eat today? Don't forget to share your experience on Instagram and Facebook and use  #DeliverooAE for a chance to win AED500 every month. Happy eating!
