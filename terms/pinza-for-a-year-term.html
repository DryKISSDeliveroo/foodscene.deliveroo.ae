Win Pinza! For a Year!

We've teamed up with PINZA! To give you the chance to create the newest menu item and win a year's supply of FREE PINZA!

Here's what you need to do: Log onto to the Deliveroo app and find the "Build Your Own PINZA" option. From there, you'll automatically be enrolled in the competition to win FREE PINZA for an entire year! Using the ingredients, the goal is to create the most imaginative and original PINZA!
